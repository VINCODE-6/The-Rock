Careers & Vacancies

Join our team and make a meaningful difference in the lives of children. We offer a supportive environment, professional development, and the joy of seeing young lives transformed.

Social Worker (Full-Time)

Requirements: Master's in Social Work (MSW), registration with SACSSP, and experience working with children and families.

Responsibilities: Case management, counseling, family reunification support, and program coordination.

Caregiver / Child and Youth Care Worker (Full-Time & Part-Time)

Requirements: Passion for children; experience or qualification in child care preferred; training provided for suitable candidates.

Responsibilities: Daily care, emotional support, activity facilitation, and creating a nurturing home environment.

Educator / Facilitator at Ikhaya Educare (Part-Time)

Requirements: ECD qualification (NQF Level 4/5), creativity, and love for early childhood development.

Responsibilities: Leading play-based learning, arts & crafts, and preparing children for Grade R.

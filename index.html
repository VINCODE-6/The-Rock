About Us

Many of the children who come to The Rock have not had the opportunity to grow and develop in a stable, nurturing environment. For this reason, a healthy and nutritious diet, age-appropriate physical activity, and strong psychological development are core to everything we do.

In partnership with Ikhaya Educare Center, we run a weekly crèche from 08:00 to 16:30, providing structured early childhood development that supports children mentally, physically, emotionally, and socially in a safe and caring environment.

Our programme is divided into four age-appropriate groups:

  • Group A: 0 to 18 months
  • Group B: 18 months to 3 years
  • Group C: 3 years to 6 years
  • Group D: 6 years to 18 years

At The Rock Children's Home, we are committed to laying strong foundations — offering not just care, but hope, stability, and the opportunity for every child to thrive.
